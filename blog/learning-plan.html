LEARNING PLAN

CAREER PATHWAY

I plan to stay in my current job in aviation for five years. With excess spare time due to my job, I want to develop remote-working skills through Dev Academy. My ideal scenario is freelancing or contract work. I intend to serve 5 more years at my current job and then reassess if I want to continue in aviation or pivot to tech. I have a BSc in Biotechnology and Chemistry so I would need to decide whether to work in software, biotechnology or drug development.

STRENGTHS & LIMITATIONS IN LEARNING

I learn quickly and think logically but get bored easily, so I prefer hands-on learning over lectures. My biggest challenge is an inconsistent schedule, which makes structured study difficult.

NON-TECHNICAL SKILLS TO DEVELOP

Task optimisation—I often feel my days slip away without accomplishing everything I intend. I want to be more diligent in not having leisure time until all my tasks are achieved.

WORKLOAD MANAGEMENT COMMITMENT

I work best in the mornings after proper rest and avoid studying after my overnight shifts. My approach is to 'just do it,' with a coffee and a quiet space. I’ll complete most work while in New Zealand, but sometimes overseas if my roster is particularly hectic.

When overwhelmed, I request extensions early, clear non-essential tasks, and prioritise study (bar work). I rarely use extensions but asking for them reduces stress.

SEEKING HELP

I’ll proactively communicate if my roster or personal life affects my ability to do quality work. I see asking for help as normal and beneficial to both the students and teachers.

EXPECTATIONS FROM FACILITATION TEAM

The team is approachable and responsive, which I appreciate—something I wish I had at university.
