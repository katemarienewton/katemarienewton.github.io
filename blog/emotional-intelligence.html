HOW IS EQ DIFFERENT TO IQ?

EQ is intelligence in regard to human emotion and connection. How to enhance happiness, group morale and to honour your gut feelings. IQ is intelligence on technology and other academic settings. How to enhance the functionality of things, connecting dots between abstract theories. Unrelated to emotions or group morale.

WHY IS EQ IMPORTANT?

If you and your team are happy, you work better. If you and your team are unhappy, you work worse. Happiness boosts productivity and improves quality of life. Unhappiness depletes energy and creates isolation. It lowers quality of life.
